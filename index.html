How It Works

Simple. Structured.
Accountable.

A straightforward four-step process — no unnecessary complexity, no surprise costs.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation — not a pitch deck. We listen to your goals, your audience, your timeline, and your budget. Everything we build is based on what you tell us here.

02

Strategy & Creator Shortlist

Within five business days, we deliver a written campaign strategy alongside a vetted creator shortlist — with audience data, engagement metrics, and proposed deliverables for your approval.

03

Campaign Execution

Once approved, we manage all creator communications, content reviews, and posting schedules — with weekly status updates and your final approval on every piece of content before it publishes.

At campaign close, you receive a full performance report — actual numbers, honest analysis, and clear recommendations for what to do next. No spin. Just results.
